Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is one of India's most successful and stylish cricketers. Known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, he is admired for his elegant stroke play and ability to score big hundreds. Born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, Rohit made his international debut in 2007.

He is the only player in the world to have scored three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs), with the highest individual ODI score of 264 runs. Rohit is also the captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats as of 2025.

Apart from his international achievements, Rohit has been a vital part of the Mumbai Indians franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL), leading them to multiple titles as captain. His calm demeanor and sharp cricketing mind make him a true asset to Indian cricket.
